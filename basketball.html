Basketball equipment

One of the most important pieces of equipment in a basketball game are the sneakers. In order to facilitate players' movement, jumping, and turning around the court, they are made to offer support, cushioning, and flexibility. Depending on their needs and personal preferences, several players may select different styles of shoes.

basketball court

The International Basketball Federation (FIBA) and other basketball organizations specify the size and requirements for a basketball court, which is typically a rectangular space.
-The dimensions of a typical indoor basketball court are 15 meters (or 49.2 feet) in width and 28 meters (or 91.9 feet) in length.
-Penalty line: The free throw line that extends to both ends of the court from the basket in order to make free throws.
-Center line: To divide the field into its two halves, a center line is drawn through the middle of the field.
-Wooden flooring: Wooden flooring is a common feature of indoor basketball courts. It offers excellent flexibility and reflection, facilitating player mobility and control.

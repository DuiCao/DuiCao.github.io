What is Hotpot?

One type of cooking that comes from China is called hotpot.

involving the dining table with a pot of soup stock cooking,

comprising a range of ingredients and meals from East Asia.

Why People Love Hotpot?

  • It's really group-oriented and social.
  • It provides a range of tastes and components.
  • It is adaptable to personal preferences.
  • The dining experience is lively and engaging.

Popular Hotpot Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced meats
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Mushrooms
  • Noodles
  • Seafood
